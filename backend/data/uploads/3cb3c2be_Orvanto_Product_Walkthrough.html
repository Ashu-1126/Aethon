Orvanto's outreach is omnichannel: if a lead is silent on one channel, it escalates to the next — spaced out and compliant. Channel access depends on the plan — Starter = Email only; Growth & Pro = all five.

Primary channel · all plans
  • How: AI writes a personalized email per lead; sent on a warmed, authenticated domain.

💳 Billing & Plan Gating

How money flows and what each plan unlocks

💳 How the Billing Channel works

👤 Signup + card → ⏱ 7-day trial · no charge → ✓ Day-8 first charge → 💳 Monthly recurring
  • Secure provider with verified, signed payment events advancing the account state.
  • One-time setup fee: $3,000 flat across paid plans (separate from the monthly price).
  • Coupons: first month free (or discounted), then full price — not free forever.
  • Plan changes: self-serve in Settings; the subscription amount updates at the next cycle.
  • Cancellation: self-service; access continues until the period ends.
  • Dunning & refunds: failed-payment retries + abandoned-payment recovery emails; refunds supported.

🔒 How Plan Gating works

  • Single source of truth defines every plan's features + limits — read by the dashboard, the runtime, and enforced server-side.
  • Two gating dimensions:
DimensionWhat it controls
QuantitativeLeads per day · number of ideal-customer profiles · channels allowed
Feature on/offWhich capabilities (channels, intelligence, Pro tools) the plan includes
  • Enforced both sides: the UI hides/locks features; the backend enforces the real caps so limits can't be bypassed.
  • "Available on X and above" → locked features show the minimum plan that unlocks them.
  • Admin override per client for bespoke deals.
